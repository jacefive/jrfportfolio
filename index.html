DraftBuddy

DraftBuddy is a Chrome extension I built that gives LinkedIn a private memory for your professional relationships and drafts replies in your own voice. Plus I gave it the classic style of the Sega Master System, because why just be helpful when you can be helpful AND stylish?

  • Auto-detects whose profile you're on and pulls in their details.
  • Logs your history with each person: a private, dated record that lives right alongside their profile.
  • Drafts replies and posts in your voice, and gets sharper the more you use it.
  • Learns your style from writing samples you upload, so it sounds like you from the start.
  • Bring your own AI key (Anthropic or OpenAI). It never auto-sends. You press the button.
  • Local-first: everything stays in your browser.

Status: in progress. Everything is working in its current form, but I'm always open to updates.

It's a personal build in developer mode, not on the Chrome Web Store. Want to try it? Email me and I'll send it your way.
